Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how williamortiz.dev ("the Site") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit and interact with the Site. Your privacy matters, and I am committed to handling your data responsibly.

Information Collected

Contact Form

When you submit the contact form, the following information is collected:

  • Name — to address you in my response
  • Email address — to reply to your inquiry
  • Message content — to understand your inquiry

This information is transmitted via Resend and delivered to my email inbox. It is not stored in a database, sold, or shared with third parties.

Automatically Collected Information

The Site may collect standard web server log data including your IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited. This data is collected by the hosting provider (Cloudflare) and is used solely for site performance and security monitoring.

How Your Information Is Used

  • To respond to your contact form submissions
  • To monitor and improve site performance and security
  • To comply with legal obligations if required

Third-Party Services

The Site uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Pages — hosting and serverless functions (Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — email delivery for the contact form (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — web fonts (Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub API — fetching public repository data (Privacy Policy)

Cookies

This Site does not use cookies or tracking scripts. No analytics or advertising trackers are present.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in my email inbox only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. No long-term database storage of form data is maintained.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about what data has been collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact me at contact@williamortiz.dev.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at contact@williamortiz.dev.
